When a Custom Quote is Needed:

For projects that involve more extensive work, specialized materials, or unique challenges, a custom on-site quote will be provided. This ensures accuracy and fair pricing for both parties. Examples include:

  • Extensive Mold or Water Damage: Requiring significant remediation before sealing.
  • Large-Scale Projects: Such as whole-house exterior caulking or major brick/mortar repair.
  • Difficult Access: Jobs requiring tall ladders, scaffolding, or complex safety measures.
  • Specialty Sealants/Colors: If specific, high-cost, or custom-colored materials are requested.
  • Structural Repairs: Any work beyond simple cosmetic touch-ups to mortar.
